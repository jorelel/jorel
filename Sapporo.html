Sapporo, Japan

 Sapporo is my dream place because of its beautiful snowy landscapes, vibrant city life, and delicious cuisine. I want to explore the city, enjoy the seasonal festivals, and experience the rich culture of Hokkaido. Located in the northern part of Japan’s Hokkaido Island, Sapporo is famous for its snow festivals, hot springs, and incredible food scene. Sapporo offers a unique mix of modern urban life and natural beauty. Whether you want to visit the famous Sapporo Snow Festival, enjoy Sapporo in winter, explore the lush parks, or savor the region's famous ramen, Sapporo has something for everyone.

Other places to visit

  • Kanazawa – Known for its well-preserved Edo-era districts, beautiful gardens, and traditional crafts.
  • Fukuoka – Famous for its vibrant food scene, ancient temples, and modern shopping areas.
  • Hakone – A relaxing destination known for its hot springs, views of Mount Fuji, and beautiful lake cruises.
  • Kyoto – A cultural hub filled with ancient temples, shrines, and traditional tea houses.
  • Osaka – A bustling city known for its vibrant nightlife, modern architecture, and delicious street food.
  • Nara – Home to friendly deer and historic sites, including the famous Todai-ji Temple.
  • Hiroshima – Known for its Peace Memorial Park and beautiful islands like Miyajima.
  • Nikko – A UNESCO World Heritage Site with stunning shrines and beautiful natural landscapes.
